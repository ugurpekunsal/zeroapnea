I Need a Machine

    If you have been diagnosed with sleep apnea, have a valid prescription and cannot afford therapy (no insurance, fixed income), we can add you to our wait list to receive a refurbished sleep apnea machine. To be added to our waitlist, please contact M. Teague email info@zeroapnea.org You will need to do three things:

  1. Provide us with a signed waiver form - you can download it HERE

  2. Provide us with a valid prescription from your doctor (an RX)

  3. Agree that if you accept a machine from the Zeroapnea.org and no longer use that machine, you will donate it back

We will make the trip to pick-up your CPAP or BiPAP machine and provide you with a disclaimer and limitation of liability clause as part of your donation.
